    4 to 2" is standard and widely used all over Michigan. Thicker covers require more clearance behind the spa to operate cover lifts properly.
    Note: dashed hinge location prior to choosing a diagram.
    Measure to the next 1/4" example: If your spa measures 88 5/8" round up to 88 3/4". Spa Tec does recommend to add 1" more to the (A & B) measurements.
    Skirt length = from top of spa vessel to bottom where shell meets the cabinet or floor(if sunk in). No skirt is available for Spas sunk flat. Skirt is split in corners to avoid bunching.
    Measurement other can be further descibed in the comments box.
    Lock Straps keep the cover closed in mild wind. For heavy wind Spa Tec recommends ratcheting type straps over entire cover.
    Cover Handles are not required with a spa cover lift.
